04 — Research

Published work

📄 IEEE Conference Paper · 2025

AgriRakshak: A Crop Disease Prediction Model Leveraging Deep Learning Techniques

2025 3rd International Conference on IoT Communication and Automation Technology (ICICAT)

A hybrid deep learning model combining Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) with Vision Transformer (ViT) architecture for automated plant disease classification from leaf images. Advanced spectral feature extraction techniques (ExG and ExR indices) were applied to improve detection accuracy and model interpretability over baseline approaches.
